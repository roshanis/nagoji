## Prose Quality Assessment

The manuscript maintains consistent voice throughout. Nagoji's first-person narration is:
- **Distinctive** without being mannered
- **Restrained** in emotional moments (trusts the reader)
- **Tactile** in physical descriptions
- **Thoughtful** without being preachy

## Thematic Coherence

Today's additions strengthen existing themes:

1. **Loyalty and Service** - Duarte's 11-year penance, all three "servants" in the chapel
2. **The Cost of State-Building** - Senior Rani's "fourteen years keeping him alive"
3. **Identity Transformation** - Duarte's journey mirrors Nagoji's
4. **Religious Complexity** - Catholic chapel in Hindu fort, blessed by the kingdom that surrendered to Padmanabha
5. **Matrilineal Power** - Senior Rani's coin, her threat, her understanding

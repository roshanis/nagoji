# Publisher Submission Packages

This folder contains submission packages for various publishers, tailored to each publisher's specific requirements.

---

## Penguin India

**Folder:** `penguin_india/`

**Requirements Met:**
- Covering letter
- Author bio
- Synopsis (detailed chapter breakdown)
- 3 sample chapters (Chapters 1-3)

**Files:**
- `covering_letter.md` - Formal cover letter
- `author_bio.md` - Author biography
- `synopsis.md` - Full synopsis with chapter breakdown
- `sample_chapters.md` - Chapters 1-3 combined

**Submission:** Email to editorial team

---

## Niyogi Books

**Folder:** `niyogi_books/`

**Requirements Met:**
- Short synopsis (max 750 words) with key arguments and uniqueness
- Broad storyline for fiction
- 2-3 sample chapters
- Target readership and market potential with competing titles
- Author CV

**Files:**
- `synopsis_750_words.md` - Condensed synopsis (under 750 words)
- `market_analysis.md` - Target readership, market potential, competing titles
- `author_cv.md` - Author CV with relevant background
- `sample_chapters.md` - Chapters 1-3

**Note:** Do not submit full manuscript until invited.

---

## HarperCollins India

**Folder:** `harpercollins_india/`

**Requirements Met:**
- Synopsis
- First three sample chapters
- Contact information in email body (name, email, phone)

**Files:**
- `email_body.md` - Template for submission email
- `synopsis.md` - Full synopsis
- `sample_chapters.md` - Chapters 1-3

**Submission:** Email with attachments

---

## Pegasus Publishers

**Folder:** `pegasus/`

**Requirements Met:**
- Synopsis with complete manuscript
- Word count
- Genre and target audience
- Author name and contact details
- Author information

**Files:**
- `submission_package.md` - Combined synopsis, author info, submission details

**Special Requirements:**
- Pegasus wants the COMPLETE manuscript (not just sample chapters)
- Must be submitted as a single Word document (.docx)
- 12pt Times New Roman font
- Convert `manuscript_v2.md` to Word format before submission

**Submission:** submissions@pegasuspublishers.com
